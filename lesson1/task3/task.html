It's not only integers and floats that have operators, booleans have them too. You might have used them before in maths. Equal to (==), not equal to (!=), greater than (>), less than (<), greater than or equals to (>=) and less than or equals to (<=).
Note that == is different from the = we use when assigning value to variables. When we use =, we are telling the computer "This IS equal to that". When we use ==, we are asking the computer "Is this equal to that?".
