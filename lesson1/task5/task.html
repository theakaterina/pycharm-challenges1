99 bottles of beer on the wall, 99 bottles of beer. You take one down, pass it around, 98 bottles of beer on the wall.
Obviously it would be really boring to write out all the lyrics to this song, so let's make the computer do it for us!
Use for loops, if/else statements, range, comparisons and print statements to get your computer to sing.

Remember:
* You have slightly different lyrics when there is only 1 bottle on the wall.
* To print multiple things, separate them by commas eg. print variable1, variable2, variable3
* You can change variables in the print statement! eg. If number = 5, print number, number - 1, number - 2 will print 5 4 3.
